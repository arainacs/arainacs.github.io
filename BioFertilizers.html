MICRO ‘N’

There are six essential Micro Nutrients for Soil: Zn, Fe, Cu, B, Mo, Mg. We are offering Same six contents in Liquid form with High percentage.

Application Soil Conditioner. Boron(B) Important in Sugar transport, cell Division and Amino Acid production. Copper(CU) component of enzymes involved with photosynthesis. Ferrus(F) essential for chlorophyll synthesis. Molybdenum(Mo) Involved in Nitrogen Metabolism. Manganese(Mn) Chloroplast Production, cofactor in many plant Reactions, activates enzymes. Zinc (Zn) is very important for Plant Hormone Balance and Auxin Acitivity.

Dosage: 500 ml per Acre for Soil Application and Drenching purpose. 3 ml Per 1 Liter of Water for Foliar Spray.

Target Crops: Cotton, Cabbage, Cauliflower, Maize, Groundnut, Tomato, Okra ,Brinjal and all other Vegetables, Cereals, Pulses. Paddy, Wheat, Tobacco, Fruits and All kind of Agricultural And Horticultural Crops.
